ExternalShearÔÉÅ

Set the potential profile to the type external shear and convergence.

This potential takes 3 parameters:

  • gamma, the strength of the external shear, constant over the field of view

  • angle_pos [in degrees], the angle of the shear direction against horizontal

  • kappa, the strength of the external convergence, constant over the field of view
